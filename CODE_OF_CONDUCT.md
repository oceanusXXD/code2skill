# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to keep discussion respectful, direct, and constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- focus on technical substance
- assume good intent
- give actionable review feedback
- accept correction without escalation

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- repeated bad-faith disruption
- publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and discussions that violate this policy.
